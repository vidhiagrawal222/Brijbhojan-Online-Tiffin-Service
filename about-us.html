About Us

BRIJ BHOJAN is one of Mathura's first online Tiffin Service providers formed by Satyam Agrawal



  • Our Vision

Our vision is to provide healthy and affordable meal delivery service to each individual daily.

At BRIJ BHOJAN we understand that in the current lifestyle we are all health conscious and yet our busy schedule keeps us from having a well balanced meal to maintain health and well being so we serve to close this gap. We follow strict quality and health standards to deliver meals which would help you keep fit and fine. We believe in doing what we do the best – making quality wholesome lip smacking meal after meal.

We invite you to taste and feel the difference.

  1. We serve absolutely affordable fresh quality food at a minimal price of Rs 65.
  2. We use quality sunflower oil for cooking, Aashirwad atta, rice and grains so that you get all healthy elements in your daily meals.
  3. Customized menu options to suit your requirements.
  4. Fresh vegetables used for all our preparations.
  5. We don’t use any canned / frozen foods in our recipes.
  6. We don’t use any pre cooked gravy all our preparations are made fresh.
  7. We take pride in Quality Standard implemented in our facility and we welcome you all to visit us.
  8. We offer Free Delivery across Mathura by our own dedicated Delivery vans for Tiffin supply. No hidden charges.
  9. We cook Veg preperations.
